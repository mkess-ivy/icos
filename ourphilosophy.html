Our Philosophy

Welcome to ICOS! A human services organization designed to help our communities and its individuals.

Mission – "Improving Today, For a Better Tomorrow"

Intensive Community Outreach Services was founded in 2008 with the goals to impact the community through increased self worth, leadership, and social connectedness. ICOS provides treatment planning and training programs to assist clients in maintaining and enhancing their level of functioning in home, school and community. ICOS provides therapeutic guidance, training and measurable goals while working with clients, family and community services to help each client meet their needs.

Programs

In-Home & School-Based Services

Intensive In-Home counselors provide clinical intervention and support services to children and youth ranging from 5 to 18 years of age.

Mentoring Program- Coming Soon

ICOS is working to develop their newly created Mentoring Program which will be geared towards children and youth.

Community-Based & Mental Health Support Services

ICOS works to support individuals in an effort to help maintain and enhance their lives.
